Academics

At Fatih College, our educational framework is anchored in the Ghana Education Service curriculum, ensuring alignment with national standards while fostering a robust academic foundation. Our commitment to academic excellence is evident in our approach to preparing students for key milestones. JHS students engage in comprehensive preparation for the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE), while their SHS counterparts are guided towards success in the West African Senior Secondary Certificate Examination (WASSCE). Moreover, our SHS program offers specialized tracks in General Science and General Arts, enabling students to pursue their passions and interests at a higher level.
