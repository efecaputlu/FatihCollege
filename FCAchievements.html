Achievements

Fatih College takes immense pride in the accomplishments of our alumni, who have gone on to excel in various fields, including medicine, engineering, nursing, education, and more. Our commitment to nurturing bright minds translates into remarkable achievements in the BECE and WASSCE, where we consistently achieve a 100% pass rate in most years. These achievements stand as a testament to our dedication to providing academic prowess that equips students for success beyond the classroom.
