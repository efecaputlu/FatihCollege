Enrollment Requirements

At Fatih College, we uphold rigorous standards for admission into our esteemed Junior High School (JHS) and Senior High School (SHS) classrooms. Prospective students are required to successfully complete the scholarship entrance test, a comprehensive assessment that evaluates not only their intellectual quotient (IQ) but also their proficiency in Science, Mathematics, and English. This meticulous selection process ensures that our student body is composed of individuals who demonstrate both academic potential and a strong foundation in key subjects.
